About

The Budget shop started as a rant by the pastry shop near Balogun market on a rainy thursday morning. Bimpe and I were looking for a shop that offers it all, class, style, quality, low price and accesibility. Oh, we spent the whole morning searching the internet for a shop mthat can offer us all these but we found none. We were frustrated as we the rains poured down and our morning went by, wasted.

We got home later that day and decided we were going to bridge this gap that was evidently visible and being ignored by almost everyone so we opened a shop, that meets all the needs you can think of and pay you as you shop. Yes!! You read that correct, the more you shop, the more points you gather for our memebership card to get discounts. So dearies, shop away, enjoy every bit of the experience and don't hesistate to send us a feedback. We would love to hear from you.
